Oappso Loyalty Integrations

Digital Loyalty Stamp Cards for your business, allowing you to automatically stamp and reward from many applications. Requires an Oappso Loyalty account.

Triggers

Actions

Reward & Reset a Customers Loyalty Card.
This action rewards and resets your customers card if they have a loyalty card with the same email address and have earned enough stamps.
Apply a Stamp to a Customers Loyalty Card.
This action applies a stamp to your customer if they have a loyalty card with the same email address.
