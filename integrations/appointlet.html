Appointlet Integrations

Appointlet makes it easy to schedule & prepare for meetings with your customers.

Triggers

Attendee Cancelled
Triggers when an attendee cancels.
Attendee Approved
Triggers when an attendee scheduling request is approved.
Attendee Declined
Triggers when an attendee scheduling request is declined.
Attendee Reminded
Triggers after a reminder is sent to an attendee.
Attendee Scheduled
Triggers when an attendee schedules a meeting or joins an existing one.

Actions

Find Attendee
Finds an attendee.

Finds a meeting.
