mailXpert Integrations

mailXpert is a leading E-Mail-Marketing Solution based in Switzerland.

Triggers

Contact Unsubscribed
Triggers when a contact is unsubscribed.
Contact Created/Updated
Triggers when a new contact is created or updated.
Email Link Opened
Triggers when an link is opened.
Email Opened
Triggers when an email is opened.

Actions

Add Contact to an Automation Flow
Add contact to an automation Flow.
Subscribe a Contact
Subscribes a contact with a double opt-in process according to the contact list settings.
Unsubscribe a Contact
Unsubscribes a contact.
Create/Update a Contact
Creates or updates a contact. If a matching contact is found then it will be updated, otherwise a new contact will be created in the selected contact list.
Create/Update a Contact and Add It to a Flow
Creates or updates a contact. If a matching contact is found then it will be updated, otherwise a new contact will be created in the selected contact list. The affected Contact will be added to the selected automation Flow.
Find a Contact
Find a contact from the email address and contact list.
