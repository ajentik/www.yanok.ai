ThunderTix Integrations

Thundertix is an all-in-one event ticketing platform to integrate your event ticketing, guest lists, customer data, barcode for fraud prevention and even order and donor histories to easily manage your events.

Triggers

New Ticket Sold
Triggers for each ticket sold. If there are multiple tickets in an order, the trigger will fire once for each ticket in the order.
New Customer
Triggers every time a customer is created.
New Gift Card Sold
Triggers when a gift card is purchased.
New MoneyTransaction
Triggers every time a money transaction is made.
New Order
Triggers every time a purchase is made.
New Order Coupon
Triggers every time a purchase is made, send coupons information.
New Order Donation
Triggers every time a purchase is made, send donations information.
New Order Giftcard
Triggers every time a purchase is made, send giftcards redeemed information.
New Order Product
Triggers every time a purchase is made, send products information.
New Order Ticket
Triggers every time a purchase is made, send tickets information.
