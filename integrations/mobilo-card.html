Mobilo Card Integrations

The Smart Business Card. Share your contact details with a tap and turn your meetings into leads. The only digital business card with data analytics and link tracking.

Triggers

New Lead (For Your Account)
Triggers when a new lead has been created for your own account.
New Lead (For Your Group)
Triggers when a new lead has been created for your group (or department), within the organization.
New Lead (For Your Organization)
Triggers when a new lead is created for any employee in your organization. If you just want to set this up for your own account, use the New Lead (for your account) trigger.
