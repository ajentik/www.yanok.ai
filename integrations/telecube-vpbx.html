TeleCube VPBX Integrations

TeleCube is a VoIP operator that delivers innovative telecommunication solutions to Business Clients. Through the modern and intuitive Client Panel, TeleCube allows easy management of calls and telecommunication services.

Triggers

SIP Account
Triggers when SIP accounts are created or deleted.
Call
Triggers when telephone connections are generated by the VPBX.
Number
Triggers when PSTN numbers are added or removed.
SIP Account Status
Triggers when the status of a SIP account changes.
System INFO
Triggers when errors occur in other events and send information to the user.
