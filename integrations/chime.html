Chime Integrations

Chime is a product of the technology company Chime Technologies, which offers a complete tech solution for real estate agents.

Triggers

Lead Group Changed
Triggers when a lead is moved to the selected group.
Lead Pipeline Changed
Triggers when a lead is moved to the selected pipeline.
New Lead
Triggers when a new lead is assigned to you. Does not work for lender-type accounts.
Smart Plan Action
Trigger this zap when a specific Smart Plan step with a unique "Zap Trigger ID" is run for a lead.

Actions

Create Lead
Creates a new lead.
Update Lead
Updates an existing lead.
