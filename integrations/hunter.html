Hunter Integrations

An easy tool to find and verify professional email addresses.

Triggers

Email Read
Triggers when an email you sent within the campaign is read.
Email Replied
Triggers when an email you sent within the campaigns gets a reply.
New Campaign
Triggers when a new campaign is available to your account.
New Lead
Triggers when a new lead is created.

Actions

Create Lead
Creates a new lead.
Create Recipient
Adds a recipient to one of your ongoing campaigns.
Count Email
Counts how many email addresses we have for a domain or a company.
Find Author
Finds the author of the article from its URL, and their most likely email address.
Find Email
Generates the most likely email address from a domain name or a company name, a first name and a last name.
Verify Email
Checks if a given email address is deliverable and has been found on the Internet.
