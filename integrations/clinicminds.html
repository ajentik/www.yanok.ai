Clinicminds Integrations

Clinicminds is the leading practice and patient management software for private healthcare clinics.

Triggers

Appointment Cancelled
Triggers when an appointment is cancelled or deleted.
Appointment Scheduled
Triggers when a new appointment is scheduled, or an existing appointment is rescheduled.
Patient Visited
Triggers when a record is closed, or an appointment is marked as ‘occurred’.
Invoice Created
Triggers when a treatment, product sale, or treatment plan/quote is invoiced, or an existing invoice is updated or credited.
Treatment Performed
Triggers when a treatment is invoiced.

Actions

Create Patient
Creates a new patient.
Find Appointments
Finds appointments of a patient.
Find Patient
Finds a patient.
Find or Create Patient
Find or creates a patient.
