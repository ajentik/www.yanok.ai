Digs Integrations

Digs provides branded financial products for real estate professionals that help capture, qualify and retain prospects.

Triggers

Saver Event
Triggers when a new saver is added to your account or when a saver updates important details in their personal profile.
Saver Property Event
Triggers when a property is added by one of your savers or when important, property-related details are updated.
Savings Fund Event
Triggers when a savings fund is added by one of your savers or when important, savings fund-related details are updated.

Actions

Find a Saver
Search for a saver by email or Digs Saver ID.
Find a Saver Property
Search for a property by Digs Saver email, Digs Saver ID or property ID.
Find a Savings Fund
Search for a property by Digs Saver email, Digs Saver ID or savings fund ID.
