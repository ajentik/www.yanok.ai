Nomics Crypto Integrations

Nomics crypto is a cryptocurrency market data provider. Get current & historical crypto price and exchange data for Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc.

Triggers

New All Time High
Triggers when the specified currency hits a new all time high (reports once daily)
New Candle (Aggregated)
Triggers when a new aggregated kline candle is opened (reports either every 24 hours or 1 hour)
New Currency Ticker (Daily)
Triggers when new data is pulled for a specified currency, this trigger updates once daily at 0:00 UTC
Rank Change
Triggers when an assets rank changes or has been unchanged for 24 hours (0:00 UTC update)
Percent Change
Triggers when the specified currency has a price movement greater than or equal to the percentage parameter (in both directions), this trigger reports either daily or hourly
