Snappy Integrations

Snappy is a help desk application for managing your email, providing customer self service and efficiently managing all your customer service tickets.

Triggers

New Ticket
Triggers when a new ticket is added.
New Unassigned Ticket
Triggers on any new, unassigned tickets from a mailbox.
New Waiting Ticket Assigned to Me
Triggers on and waiting ticket assigned to you.
New Wall Post
Triggers when a new post is added to the team wall.

Actions

Create Wall Post
Creates a post on the team wall.
Create Note As Customer
Creates a note as the customer.
Create Note To Customer
Creates a note to the customer.
