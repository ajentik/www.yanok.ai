GoodBarber eCommerce Integrations

GoodBarber is a no-code app building platform to create iOS and Android native apps. With GoodBarber eCommerce, create and manage your online store to sell your products on mobile.

Triggers

New Abandonned Order
Triggers when a new order is abandonned before purchase.
New Customer
Triggers when a new customer is created.
New Order
Triggers when a new order is successfully created.
New Product
Triggers when a new product is created.
New Product Variant
Triggers when a new variant is created.
New Promocode
Triggers when a new promocode is created.
New Lead
Triggers when a new lead is created.
Updated Promocode
Triggers when a promocode is updated.

Actions

Update a Tracking URL
Updates the parcel tracking URL associated with an order.
Send Push Notification
Sends a push notification to every recipients.
Update the Inventory Quantity
Updates the quantity of an existing product variant.
Find a Variant
Finds a variant by ID.
Find a Collection
Finds a collection by ID.
Find an Option
Finds an option by ID.
Find a Product
Finds a product by ID.
