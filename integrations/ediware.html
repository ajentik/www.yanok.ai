Ediware Integrations

Ediware is an email and SMS marketing platform. This integration enables users to pull and retrieve data and behaviors with their CRM.

Triggers

Subscriber Deleted
Triggers when a contact is deleted from the list.
New Hard Bounce
Triggers when there is a new hard bounce on an account.
New Subscriber
Triggers when a contact is added is the list.
Subscriber Updated
Triggers when a contact from the list is updated.
New Unsubscribe
Triggers when an email address unsubscribe from an account.

Actions

Create Subscriber
Creates a new contact in a list.
Delete Subscriber
Delete a contact from a list.
Update Subscriber
Update a contact from a list.
