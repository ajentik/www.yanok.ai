AidaForm Integrations

AidaForm is a SaaS solution allowing you to create and publish online forms, Instagram Bio links, and landing pages. Collect and analyze response data, process and share the data with other services and tools.

Triggers

New File
Triggers when your specified form receives every single uploaded file.
New Payment
Triggers when AidaForm receives a payment notification for the specified form.
New Response
Triggers when your specified form receives a new response.
