LeadLander Integrations

LeadLander is a website tracking tool that can identify companies and people visiting your website to help grow your sales pipeline.

Triggers

New Company Visit
Triggers when a new company is identified by LeadLander that has not visited before
New Person Visit
Triggers when a new person is identified visiting the website
Repeat Company Visit
Triggers when a new company is identified by LeadLander that has visited before
Specific Page Visit
Triggers when a specific page is visited on a LeadLander customer's website
Visit Pages Count
Triggers when a specified number of pages have been viewed for a visit.
