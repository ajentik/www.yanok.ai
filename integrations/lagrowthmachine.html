LaGrowthMachine Integrations

LaGrowthMachine let Sales team get more meetings. Create multi-channel workflows to engage with your leads on LinkedIn, Twitter & by Email.

Triggers

Email Clicked
Triggers when a lead clicked on a link in one of your emails. Click tracking must be turned on.
Email Opened
Triggers when a lead opened one of your emails.
Email Replied
Triggers when a lead replied to one of your emails.
Email Sent
Triggers when an email is sent to a lead.
Goal Reached
Triggers when a lead is marked as converted.
Linkedin Contact Request Accepted
Triggers when a lead accepted your identity's connection request on LinkedIn.
Linkedin Contact Request Sent
Triggers when a LinkedIn contact request is sent to a lead.
Linkedin Message Replied
Triggers when a lead replied to one of your LinkedIn messages or an invite note.
Linkedin Message Sent
Triggers when a LinkedIn message is sent to a lead.
Linkedin Profile Visited
Triggers when a lead's LinkedIn profile is visited.
Profile Enriched
Triggers when a Lead is enriched by LGM.
Sequence Ended
Triggers when the lead finishes a campaign sequence.
Twitter DM Sent
Triggers when a Twitter DM is sent to a lead.
Twitter Favorite Tweet
Triggers when a lead's tweet is favorited.
Twitter Followed
Triggers when a lead is followed on Twitter.
Twitter Retweeted
Triggers when a lead's tweet is retweeted.
Twitter Tweeted
Triggers when a tweet is sent.
Twitter Unfollowed
Triggers when a lead is unfollowed on Twitter.

Actions

Create Lead in an Audience
Create a lead in an LGM audience.
Import Leads From LinkedIn Search URL
Create an LGM audience and import leads from a LinkedIn Regular or Sales Navigator search
Remove Lead From Audience
Remove a lead from one or more LGM audiences
Update Lead Status
Change the LGM status of a specific lead in a specific campaign.
