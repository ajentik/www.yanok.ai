Agendize Integrations

Agendize is a customer engagement platform that offers easy appointment booking, contact management, live chat and call-tracking.

Triggers

New Appointment
Triggers when a new appointment is made for a specific company.
New Call
Triggers when a new call is made from Agendize tools.
New Client in CRM
Triggers when a new client is created.
New Company
Triggers when a new company is created.
New or Updated Client
Immediately triggers when a new client is created
New Form Response
Triggers when a new form response is submitted for a custom form.
New Staff
Triggers when a new staff is created.
Updated Client Account
Triggers when a new client account is created or updated in your reseller console
Updated Appointment
Triggers when an appointment status is updated (eg: accepted, no show, completed, cancelled)

Actions

Change Client Account Password
Changes the Password of the Client Account Owner
Upgrade or Downgrade Client Account
Changes the Profile ID associated with a Client Account

Creates and configure a new Client account in your reseller console, so that your Client gets a pre-configured account
Add Note for CRM Client
Adds a new note to a CRM Client
Create CRM Client
Creates a Client entry in your CRM
Create Service
Creates a new service for appointment scheduling.
Create Staff
Staff to be created for a scheduling company
Create Company
Create a company for a specific account. Set the important settings needed to start using the firm.
Create New Client Account
Creates a new Client account in your reseller console, so that your Client can configure it
Get Client Account Details
Get account details such as SSO token, owner email address...
Get Client Account Scheduling Widget Details
Retrieves Scheduling Widget URL, iframe & popin integrations from a Client Account
Schedule an Appointment
Books an appointment
Send Email-Marketing
Sends an email-marketing from a template defined in your account.

Find Next Freeslot
Finds a Freeslot
