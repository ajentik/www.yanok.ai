MyBizzMail Integrations

Mybizzmail is an email marketing service provider. With it's great drag and drop editor you can easily make newsletters, magazines and forms. MyBizzMail also gives you a report of who has visited which pages on your site via the email.

Triggers

New Subscriber
Triggers when you have a new subscriber.

Actions

Add to Group
Adds the given email to one of your groups.
Send Email to Group
Sends an existing email to one of your group of users.
Send Email to User
Sends an existing email to an email address.
