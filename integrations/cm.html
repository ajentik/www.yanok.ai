Actions

Send Text (SMS/Push) Message
Sends an SMS or Push message to one or multiple people.
Send Voice (Text to Speech) Call
Sends a voice message to one or multiple people. The recipient will receive the message as a call.
Lookup or Validate Phone Number
Searches for information (like formatting options, number type or carrier) of a phone number.
