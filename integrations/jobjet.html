Jobjet Integrations

Jobjet is a solution to quickly find & contact people.

Triggers

Person Created
Triggers when a new person profile is created.
Person Updated
Triggers when a person profile was updated, including successful enrichment.

Actions

Create & Update Person
Update a person profile in Jobjet. Creates a new person profile if we are unable to find a match with an existing one. Requires at least one piece of contact info: email, phone or social link.
