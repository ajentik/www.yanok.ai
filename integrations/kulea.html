Kulea Integrations

Kulea is a marketing automation and personalisation platform build for SMEs.

Triggers

New or Updated CRM Data
Triggers when new contact data is created or updated in one of the Data fields of Kulea. The Data fields are typically all the fields you add to your CRM.
Email Opened
Triggers when any contact opens a new email. Note that this could trigger a lot of zap tasks if you have a successful large email campaign.
New File Downloaded
Triggers whenever a file is downloaded by a contact.
Lead Score Updated
Triggers when any contact's lead score is updated. Note that this could incur quite a lot of zap tasks depending on your lead score strategy.
