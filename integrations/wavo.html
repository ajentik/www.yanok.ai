Wavo Integrations

Wavo is a cold email tool that automatically sends outbound emails and follow up sequences directly from your email account.

Triggers

Campaign Complete
Triggers when a Campaign completes.
Email Opened
Triggers when a contact opens an email.
Contact Negative
Triggers when a contact is manually marked as NEGATIVE
Contact Neutral
Triggers when a contact is manually marked as NEUTRAL.
Contact Positive
Triggers when a contact is manually marked as POSITIVE.
Contact Replied
Triggers when a contact replies or is manually marked as REPLIED.
Contact Unsubscribed
Triggers when a contact unsubscribes or is manually marked as UNSUBSCRIBED.

Actions

Add Contact
Adds a new contact to the selected campaign.
Block Contact
Adds an email or domain to your suppression list.
Create Team
Creates a new team.
Invite User to Team/Client
Sends an email invitation to user to join a team/client.
Stop Contact
Stops sending emails to a contact in a campaign.
