Home Inspector Pro Office Integrations

Home Inspector Pro Office is a tool to automate and streamline your home inspection business.

Triggers

Contract Signed
Triggers when the contract for an order is signed by a client in HIP Office.
Invoice Paid
Triggers when an invoice is paid in HIP Office.
New Agent Added
Triggers when a new real estate agent is added to HIP Office.
New Client Added
Triggers when a new client is added to HIP Office.
New Order Created
Triggers when a new Inspection order is created in HIP Office.
Completed Order
Triggers when an order is marked complete in HIP Office. This could be an order automatically or manually closed.
Report Sent
Triggers when a report is sent out via email to your clients.
Report Viewed
Triggers when a report is viewed in HIP Office.
