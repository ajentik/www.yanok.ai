Frame.io Integrations

Frame.io is a video review and collaboration platform. Share video projects with your team and clients for fast feedback and approval.

Triggers

New Asset
Trigger when a new asset is uploaded.
Deleted Asset
Triggered when an asset is deleted.
Updated Asset Label
Triggers when an Asset's status label is updated.
Asset Versioned
Trigger when an asset is versioned.
New Comment
Triggers when a new Comment is left on an Asset.
Updated Comment
Triggers when a Comment is updated.
New Project
Trigger when a new project is created.
