Chekkit Integrations

Chekkit is a review management and messaging tool for local business.

Triggers

New Inbound Text Message
Triggers when you a receive a new SMS message to your Chekkit account.
New Review Invitation Sent
Triggers when a new review invitation is sent.
New Webchat Message
Triggers when a new message is sent through your webchat widget.

Actions

Create a New Customer
Create a new customer (and optional message) in Chekkit.
Send Text Message
Sends a text message to a customer through Chekkit via SMS or Email. Text message replies will appear in your Chekkit dashboard, email replies will be sent to your email inbox.
Send a Review Invitation
Sends a review invitation to a customer via sms or email.
