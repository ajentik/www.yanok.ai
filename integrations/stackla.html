Stackla Integrations

Stackla is a social content marketing platform used by hundreds of global brands, agencies, media companies, and nonprofits to discover, curate and publish the best content from the social web.

Triggers

Asset Updated
Triggers when an Asset is updated in the Stackla Asset Manager. Requires Stackla Webhooks.
New Asset Created
Triggers when a new Asset is added to the Stackla Asset Manager. Requires Stackla Webhooks.
Send Asset or Tile
Send a Tile or Asset to a third party application. Requires Stackla Webhooks.
New Tile Claimed
Triggers when a content creator completes the Rights by Registration process.
New Tile Published
Triggers when a tile has its status changed to Publish.
New Tile Tagged
Triggers when a tile has a tag applied to it.
