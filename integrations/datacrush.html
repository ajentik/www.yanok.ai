DataCrush Integrations

DataCrush is an all-in-one Marketing & Sales Automation platform. The growth of your business requires a flexible and secure platform in your language. Create incredible Marketing actions, the limit is your imagination.

Triggers

New Contact
Triggers when a new contact is created.
Update Contact
Triggers when a contact is updated.
New Form Submission
Triggers when a form is submitted.
New Contact in List
Triggers when a contact is added to the specified list.

Actions

Add Contact to List
Adds a contact to a specific static list.
Add Contact to Workflow
Adds a contact to a specific workflow.
Create a New Contact
Create a new contact only if doesn't exists.
Create or Update a Contact
Creates a new contact or updates an existing contact based on email address.
Update an Existing Contact
Updates an existing contact based on email address.
Find Contact by Email
Find an existing contact by email address.
Search Contact
Search contacts by email, first name, last name, company or phone.
