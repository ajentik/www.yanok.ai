GoCardless Integrations

GoCardless is a payments platform helping businesses collect payments directly from customer bank accounts.

Triggers

IBP or Direct Debit Mandate: Created
Triggers when a new instant payment (Instant Bank Pay) or mandate is being created.
IBP or Direct Debit Mandate: Fulfilled
Triggers when an instant payment (Instant Bank Pay) or a mandate is successfully created.
Direct Debit Mandate: Cancelled
Triggers when a mandate is cancelled.
Payment: Failed
Triggers when a payment is failed.

Actions

Create Direct Debit Mandate
Creates Direct Debit Mandate on a customer's bank account. This is a prerequisite for creating payments against a mandate.
Create Instant Bank Payment
Creates Instant Bank Payment. Note: currently available for the UK and Germany only.
Create Payment
Creates a Payment against an existing Customer's Mandate.
