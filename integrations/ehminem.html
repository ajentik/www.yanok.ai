Ehminem Integrations

Ehminem is an app that cuts filler words from your podcast. Currently available for German.

Triggers

Podcast Ready
Triggers when a freshly cut podcast is ready to be downloaded.

Actions

Upload and Cut Podcast
Uploads and cuts a podcast automatically. Set up a trigger "Podcast ready" to handle when the podcast is ready. Attention: This action will subtract credits from your account when you have enough. The pricing is the same as in web app.
