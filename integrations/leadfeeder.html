Leadfeeder Integrations

Leadfeeder is a B2B sales tool that uncovers the companies visiting your website and what they do there. By simply inserting a snippet of code, Leadfeeder empowers sales and marketing teams to connect with the leads that matter most to their business.

Triggers

New Lead
Triggers when a new lead is tracked for the first time (leads are tracked hourly).
Recurring Lead
Triggers when a lead matching your criteria visits your site again. Visit information on the trigger contains data of the first visit during that day.

Actions

Find Lead
Find a lead in Leadfeeder by its name or website URL.
