Propago Integrations

Propago Allows Print Producers, Enterprises and Marketers To Better Manage, Personalize, Produce, and Distribute Their Marketing Assets.

Triggers

Order Events (Detailed Format)
Triggers when a the selected Event of an Order is triggered. Must have SITE or COMPANY level access and Order Permission enabled.
Order Events (Simple Format)
Triggers when a the selected Event of an Order is triggered. Must have SITE or COMPANY level access and Order Permission enabled.

Actions

Place Order - Company Only
Places an Order to the Propago System. Must have COMPANY level access and Order Permission enabled.
Search Parts
Searches Parts by many conditions. Must have SITE or COMPANY level access and Part Permission enabled.
Search Users
Search Users by Keyword
Get Company by Id - Site Only
Finds a Company by its Id. Must have SITE or COMPANY level access.
Get Part by Sku
Gets a Part by its Display Sku. Must have SITE or COMPANY level access and Part permission enabled.
Get User by Id
Gets a User by the Id. Must have SITE or COMPANY level access.
Search Orders
Searches orders using many parameters. Must have SITE or COMPANY level access and Order Permission enabled.
Search Shipments
Search for Shipments. Must have SITE or COMPANY level access and Shipment Permission enabled.
