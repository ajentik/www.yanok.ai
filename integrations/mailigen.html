Mailigen Integrations

Mailigen is a new generation of integrated marketing platform, which includes social, email and mobile marketing.

Triggers

New Campaign
Triggers when a new email campaign is created.
New Subscriber
Triggers when a new contact is added to a specified list.
New List
Triggers when a new contact list is added.
New Segment
Triggers when new segment is created.

Actions

Add/Update Subscriber by Email
Adds new or updates existing subscriber by email address.
Create Campaign
Creates a new email campaign.
Create List
Creates a new contact list.
Add Phone to List
Adds a new phone number to existing list with all additional values of the field.
Schedule Campaign
Schedules a campaign that was previously saved as a draft.
Send Campaign
Sends a campaign that was previously saved as a draft. Campaign is sent immediately.
