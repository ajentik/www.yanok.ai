Msaaq Integrations

The all-in-one platform to create and sell your online courses and digital products from your academy without any technical knowledge.

Triggers

Course Completed
Triggers when a member completed a course.
New Enrollment
Triggers when a member enrols a course.
New Member
Triggers when a new member (student or admin) is created.

Actions

Enroll Member
Enroll a member in a course. (If the person does not have an account, one will be created and they will then be enrolled.)
Find Course
Find a course at your academy by the given title.
