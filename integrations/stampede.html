Stampede Integrations

Stampede offers customer insights and effective marketing campaigns that will increase your sales and returning customers.

Triggers

Booking
Triggers when a customer creates or updates a booking.
Connection
Triggers when a customer connects to locations.
Interaction
Triggers when a customer interacts with any Stampede data source.
Validated Registration
Triggers when a new customer registers.
Unvalidated Registration
Triggers when a new user registers that hasn't validated.

Actions

Create Suppressed User
Adds a user to the suppression list.
Create Registration
Put registration data into Stampede.
