Direct Mail Integrations

Direct Mail is a native OS X app for creating, sending, and tracking email campaigns. Includes tools for building mailing list sign-up forms, autoresponders, dynamic content, and more. Works offline and online, including cloud collaboration features.

Triggers

Deleted Address
Triggers when an address is deleted from your address group.
New Address
Triggers when a new address is added to your group.
Sent Campaign
Triggers when an email campaign is sent.
Updated Address
Triggers when an address in your group is unsubscribed, hard bounced, or otherwise updated.

Actions

Delete Address
Removes an address from a group.
New Address
Creates a new address or updates an existing address.
Unsubscribe Address
Marks an address as unsubscribed and (optionally) removes it from the group.
Update Address
Update an address.
Find Address
Finds an address by email.
