My Most Trusted Network Integrations

My Most Trusted Network is a network building Chrome Extension for LinkedIn that allows its users to make business referrals and introductions in a safe and trusted way. It also allows users to link and sync LinkedIn data to a CRM.

Triggers

Invitations
Triggers when LinkedIn invitations are sent.
Invitations Accepted
Triggers when LinkedIn invitations are accepted.
Invitations Tagged
Triggers when LinkedIn invitations are tagged with specified tag.
Users
Triggers when LinkedIn users join your network.
