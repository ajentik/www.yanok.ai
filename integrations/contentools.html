Workflow by GrowthHackers Integrations

GrowthHackers Workflow is a content marketing platform to streamline and manage content creation. Create. Collaborate. Publish. All in one place.

Triggers

New Content
Triggers when a new content created and/or an idea, is sent to production in a project that you are a team member of.
Content Delayed
Triggers when the content gets behind its step's estimated finish date.
Content Finished
Triggers when a content get its production Finished, through the platform or approval email.
Content Published
Triggers in three scenarios: 1) when the scheduled date to publish your content has arrived, 2) when an idea has its production started or 3) when you hit on Publish Now in a content.
Content Step Changed
Triggers when a content moves between steps of its workflow.
Idea Approved
Triggers when an idea is approved on a project that you are member of.
Idea Suggested
Triggers when a new Idea is suggested in a project that you are a team member of.
Idea Rejected
Triggers when an Idea gets rejected in a project that you are a member of.
Idea Scheduled
Triggers when an idea gets scheduled in a project that you are a member of.

Actions

Suggest New Idea to Project
Suggests a new idea in a project that you are a team member of.
Create New Blog Post
Creates a new piece of content for Blog Post media type in your project.
Create New Social Post
Creates a new piece of content for Social Post media type in your project.
