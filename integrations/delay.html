Delay by Zapier Integrations

Put your Actions on hold for a specified amount of time before sending data to another app. You can set up scheduled emails, get notified of incomplete tasks, send automatic follow-ups, and automate other tasks on your timeline.

Triggers

Actions

Delay For
Waits for a set amount of time before completing your Actions.
Delay After Queue
Waits for a set amount of time after the last delay in a given queue. Used to deal with race conditions and rate-limiting.
Delay Until
Waits to run your Actions until a date or time that you choose.
