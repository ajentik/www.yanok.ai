Planyo Online Booking Integrations

Planyo is an online reservation system which can be used by any business taking bookings. It's used, amongst others, by businesses offering vacation properties, car rentals, hotels, driving schools, yacht charters, doctors and therapists, conference rooms, tennis courts, car parks, event tickets or any other bookings you could imagine.

Triggers

Before or After Event #1
Triggers a given number of hours/days before or after the rental begins or ends.
Before or After Event #2
Triggers a given number of hours/days before or after the rental begins or ends (#2 event defined in Notifications in the Planyo backend).
Before or After Event #3
Triggers a given number of hours/days before or after the rental begins or ends (#3 event defined in Notifications in the Planyo backend).
Before or After Event #4
Triggers a given number of hours/days before or after the rental begins or ends (#4 event defined in Notifications in the Planyo backend).
Before or After Event #5
Triggers a given number of hours/days before or after the rental begins or ends (#5 event defined in Notifications in the Planyo backend).
Before or After Event #6
Triggers a given number of hours/days before or after the rental begins or ends (#6 event defined in Notifications in the Planyo backend).
Update of Reservation Status to Checked-In
Triggers when the customer checks in.
Update of Reservation Status to Checked-Out
Triggers when the customer checks out.
New Reservation
Triggers when a new reservation is made.
Update of Reservation Status to Cancelled by Admin
Triggers when a reservation is cancelled by an admin.
Update of Reservation by Admin
Triggers when a reservation is modified by an admin.
Automatic Update of Reservation Status to Cancelled
Triggers when a reservation is automatically cancelled according to the cancellation rules set for the resource.
New Reservation Is Confirmed
Triggers when a reservation gets confirmed.
New Payment
Triggers when a new payment is made.
Update of Reservation Status to Cancelled by Customer
Triggers when a reservation is cancelled by the customer.
Update of Reservation by Customer
Triggers when a reservation is modified by the customer.
New or Updated Customer
Triggers when a new customer has been added or when customer data has been updated.

Actions

New Customer
Adds a new user (customer) to your planyo site.
Create Vacation
Adds a new vacation (period of unavailability) for given resource or entire planyo site.
Create Reservation
Enters a new reservation into the system.
