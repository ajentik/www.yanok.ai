LinkedIn Ads Integrations

LinkedIn Ads allows marketers to collect even more quality leads from their ads and events on LinkedIn with seamless pre-filled forms.

Triggers

New Event Registration Form Response
Triggers when you receive a new event response.
New Lead Gen Form Response
Triggers when you receive a new form response.

Actions

Add Email to Audience
Adds an email address to an Audience.
Create Audience
Creates an Audience that you can add email addresses to.
Remove Email from Audience
Removes an email address from an Audience.
