Hot Prospector Integrations

Hot Prospector is ideal for anyone who has an existing database of past customers or is looking to bring in a rush of brand new prospects.

Triggers

Pipeline Status Added or Removed From Lead
Triggers when a Pipeline Status is added or removed from a Lead.
Tag Added or Removed From Lead
Triggers when a Tag is added or removed from a Lead.
Lead Copied or Moved
Triggers when a Lead is copied or moved to Group.
New Group
Triggers when a new Group is created.
New Lead Added to Group
Triggers when a new Lead is added to a Group.

Actions

Add/Remove Pipeline Status From Lead
Add or remove a pipeline status from Lead
Add/Remove Tag From Lead
Add or remove a tag from Lead
Copy/Move Lead
Copy or move a Lead to group
Create or Update Lead
Create a new lead, or updates an existing lead.
Find Lead
Finds a lead by keywords like- name, email, phone, city, etc
