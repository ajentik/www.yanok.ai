Omnify Integrations

Omnify is a scheduling & business management platform which enables you to sell various services from a single platform.

Triggers

Abandoned Booking
Triggers when a booking is marked as Abandoned.
Booking Detail
Triggers when a new booking is made; forwards all the booking details in the response.
Checkins
Triggers when a new check-in happens in the business.
Class Pack Renewal - Booking
Triggers on renewal of class pack booking.
Class Pack Renewal - Invoice
Triggers when class pack renewal invoice generated in the business.
Memberships Renewal - Booking
Triggers on renewal of membership booking.
Memberships Renewal - Invoice
Triggers when membership renewal invoice is generated in the business.
New Booking
Triggers when a new booking is made.
New Client
Triggers when a new client is added to the business.
New Lead
Triggers when a new client is added to the business.
New Family Member
Triggers when a new family member is added to the business.
Invoice Paid
Triggers when paid invoice is generated in the business.
Payment Reminder
Triggers within 3 hours from when the booking is made.

Actions

Create Client
Adds new customer to your Omnify's business.
