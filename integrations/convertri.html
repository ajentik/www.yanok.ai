Convertri Integrations

Convertri is a complete sales funnel and website builder, focused on building the fastest pages you’ve ever seen.

Triggers

New Submission
Triggers when a form or checkout is submitted from anywhere in your Convertri account. You can choose whether the trigger activates on form submissions only, checkout submissions only, or both.
New Submission From Page
Triggers when a form or checkout is submitted from a specific page in your Convertri account. You can choose whether the trigger activates on form submissions only, checkout submissions only, or both.
Product Refunded
Triggers when someone refunds a product sale. You'll need to select which product.
New Product Sale
Triggers when the product selected has been sold from any sales page in your Convertri account.
New Product Sale From Page
Triggers when the product selected has been sold from a specific sales page. You'll need to select the product and the page.
New Sale of Any Product
Triggers whenever a sale is made on your Convertri account.
New Sale of Any Product From Page
Triggers when any product is sold from a specific page you've selected.
Product Subscription Cancelled
Triggers when someone cancels a subscription to a product. You'll need to select which product.
