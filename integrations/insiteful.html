Insiteful Integrations

Insiteful is a web form tracking & optimization tool to automagically unlock more leads from any form — make every click count!

Triggers

New Form
Triggers when a new form is detected and tracked.
New Form Fill
Triggers every time a user fills out any form field you are tracking.
New Form Fills (Grouped)
Triggers every time a unique visitor fills out a form you are tracking.
New Lead
Triggers when a new lead (partial form submission with contact data) is detected & captured by Insiteful.

Actions

Get Form
Get details for a Form
