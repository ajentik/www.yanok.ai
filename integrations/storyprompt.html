StoryPrompt Integrations

StoryPrompt is an easy way to send and collect personal videos from customers, employees, and your community. Capturing stories, testimonials, and updates is as simple as sharing a link.

Triggers

New Contact Details Received
Triggers when a respondent to a Prompt provides any contact details.
New Contact Phone Received
Triggers when a respondent to a Prompt provides their phone number.
New Contact Email Received
Triggers when a respondent to a Prompt provides their email address.
New Member Joined Space
Triggers when a new member joins a Space.
New Mix Produced (Any)
Triggers when a new Mix has been produced anywhere in your account.
New Mix Produced
Triggers when a Mix is produced for ONE of your public StoryPrompts.
New Prompt in a Space
Triggers when someone posts a new Prompt in a Campfire within one of your Spaces.
New Response Received
Triggers when someone responds via video or audio to ONE of your public StoryPrompts.
New Response Received (Any)
Triggers when someone responds via video or audio to ANY of your public StoryPrompts.
New Response in a Campfire
Triggers when someone posts a new Response to a Prompt in a Campfire within one of your Spaces.
