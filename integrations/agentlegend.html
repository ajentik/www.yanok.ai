Agent Legend Integrations

Agent Legend is an automated, multi-channel marketing tool.

Triggers

Contact Added to Campaign
Triggers when a contact is added to a campaign.
Contact Responded
Triggers when a contact responds to one of your campaigns.

Actions

Add Contact to Campaign
Adds a contact to a campaign.
Stop Campaign for Contact
Stops a campaign for the specified contact.
Update Contact
Updates a Contact's details.
Find Contact
Find an existing contact by email address or phone number.
