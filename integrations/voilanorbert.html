VoilaNorbert Integrations

VoilaNorbert is an easy tool to find anyone's email address and reach out to them.

Triggers

Contact Search Completed
Triggers when a search for a contact has been completed. Will trigger regardless of whether the contact was found.
Verify Email Completed
Triggeres when a new verification is completed

Actions

Start Contact Search
Starts a new search for a contact with the given email and domain. The contact will be created in your account once the search completes. You can use the "Contact Search Completed" trigger to be notified when the search has completed.
Verify Email
Verifies an emaill address
Find Contact
Find any contacts in your account.
