Zoho Campaigns Integrations

Zoho Campaigns lets you send email and social media campaigns to your subscribers.

Triggers

New Contact
Triggers when a new contact is added to a mailing list.
New Campaign
Triggers when a campaign is sent, reviewed, stopped, canceled, paused, and A/B test is complete.
Unsubscribe
Triggers when a contact unsubscribes from any of the mailing lists or an email address is removed from a mailing list by administrator.

Actions

Clone Campaign
Clones an exisiting campaign.
Create Campaign
Creates a new campaign in Zoho Campaigns.
Add/Update Contact
Adds a new contact or updates an existing contact in Zoho Campaigns. Contact will not get any confirmation email to join a mailing list.
Send Campaign
Sends a campaign.
Unsubscribe
Removes a contact from a mailing list.
Find Campaign
Searches an existing campaign.
Find Contact
Searches an existing contact.
