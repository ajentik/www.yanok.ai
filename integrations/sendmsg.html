Sendmsg (שלח מסר) Integrations

Sendmsg is an all-in-one marketing platform for newsletters providing email automation, landing pages, SMS, digital courses and more.

Triggers

Subscriber Joined a Mailing List
Triggers when a subscriber is joining a mailing list.
New Subscriber Registered by Sign-Up Form
Triggers when a new subscriber is signing up via Sendmsg form.
Subscriber Registered by Sign-Up Form
Triggers when a subscriber is signing up via Sendmsg form.
Unsubscribe
Triggers when a subscriber is unsubscribing himself from Sendmsg.

Actions

Add New Subscriber
Add new subscriber to you Sendmsg's account.
Add Subscriber to Mailing List
Add a subscriber to any of your mailing list in Sendmsg's account.
Remove Subscriber From Mailing List
Remove subscriber from one of your sendmsg's mailing list. this action will not remove the subscriber from the account, just from the mailing list.
Update Subscriber's Details
Update details of existing subscriber in your Sendmsg's account.
