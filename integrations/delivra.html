Delivra Integrations

Delivra is a robust multi-channel marketing automation platform used to keep your prospects informed through their customer journey. With intuitive data management and easy personalization tools, we make it easy to acquire, manage, and nurture leads through that other crucial period – after they’re your customer!

Triggers

Category Join
Triggers when a contact joins a specific category.
Category Unsub
Triggers when a contact unsubscribes from a specific category.
New Abandoned Cart
Triggers when a new abandoned cart is created.
New Contact
Triggers when a new contact is created.
New Order
Triggers when a new order is created.
New Unsubscribe
Triggers when contacts unsubscribe.
Updated Abandoned Cart
Triggers when an abandoned cart gets updated.
Updated Contact
Triggers when a contact gets updated.
Updated Order
Triggers when an Order is updated.

Actions

Add Contact to Category
Add a given contact (by email address or ID) to a set of categories.
Create/Update Contact
Creates a new contact or updates an existing contact based on email address. Can be used to send a Welcome or Confirm Opt-In email.
Remove Contact From Category
Remove a given contact (by email address or ID) from a category (by Name or ID).
Send Transactional/Automated Email
Send a transactional email (from your Delivra account) to this contact.
Unsubscribe Contact
Unsubscribe this contact from your list in Delivra.
Create/Update Abandoned Cart
Creates a new abandoned cart or updates if the cart already exists.
Create/Update Abandoned Cart Line Item
Creates a new abandoned cart line item or updates if the line item already exists.
Create/Update Meeting
Creates a Meeting or updates the Meeting if it already exists.
Create/Update Meeting Registrant
Creates a meeting registrant or if the meeting registrant already exists then it gets updated.
Create/Update Order
Creates a new order or updates if the order already exists.
Create/Update Order Line Item
Creates a new order line item or updates if the line item already exists.
