Offri Integrations

Offri enables you to create great looking proposals quickly.

Triggers

Contact Removed
Triggers when a client is removed.
Contact Updated
Triggers when a client is created or updated.
Contact Person Removed
Triggers when a Contact Person is removed.
Contact Person Updated
Triggers when a Contact Person is created or updated.
Proposal Opened for the First Time
Triggers when a proposal has been opened by a contact for the first time.
Proposal Removed/Hidden
Triggers when a proposal is removed or hidden.
Proposal Status Changed
Triggers when a proposal has either expired, lost, or won.
Proposal Updated
Triggers when a proposal is created or updated.

Actions

Create Contact
Creates a contact.
Create Contact Person
Creates a contact person under a contact
Create Proposal
Creates a proposal, optionally from a template.
Find and Create/Update Contact
Finds and updates a Contact, optionally creates one if it doesn't exist
Find and Create/Update Contact Person
Finds and updates a Contact Person, optionally creates one if it doesn't exist
Remove Contact
Removes a contact.
Remove Contact Person
Removes a contact person under a contact
Update Contact
Updates a contact.
Update Contact Person
Updates a contact person under a contact
Find Contact
Finds a Contact
Find Contact Person
Finds a Contact Person
Find or Create Contact
Finds a Contact, optionally creates one if it doesn't exist
Find or Create Contact Person
Finds a Contact Person, optionally creates one if it doesn't exist
