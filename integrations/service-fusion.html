Service Fusion Integrations

Service Fusion is a field service management software for service contractors. With this application, you create new customers, retrieve job and estimate history, technician information and much more.

Triggers

Job Status Updated
Triggers when a job status is updated.
New Customer
Triggers when a new Service Fusion customer is created.
New Estimate
Triggers when a new Service Fusion estimate is created.
New Invoice
Triggers when a new Service Fusion invoice is created.
New Job
Triggers when a new Service Fusion job is created.
New Tech
Triggers when a new Service Fusion tech is created.

Actions

Create Calendar Task
Creates a new Service Fusion calendar task.
Create Customer
Creates a new Service Fusion customer.
Create Job
Creates a new Service Fusion job.
