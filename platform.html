RAG

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) enhances AI capabilities by combining knowledge bases with advanced language models, ensuring accurate and contextual responses while maintaining data control.

What's RAG

A powerful approach that combines vector embeddings and semantic search with Large Language Models to enable accurate, context-aware responses based on your organization's data.

RAG Methods

Utilizes sophisticated retrieval strategies including vector databases, embedding models, and semantic search to efficiently match queries with relevant information from your knowledge base.

RAG Frameworks

Supports multiple integration patterns, custom tokenization, and flexible deployment options through standardized APIs and comprehensive monitoring capabilities.

Interaction with Agentic AI

Seamlessly integrates with autonomous AI agents, enhancing their decision-making capabilities with accurate, contextual information from your organization's knowledge base.
